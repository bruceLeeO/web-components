About Me

Hello! My name is Leonardo de Leon. Not only do I have a lot of passion for building nice looking, tasteful website, I also have a knack for solving rather complex problem. I strive for excellence in what I do and that is why I am currently going through the rigorous Fullstack Engineering course work at BOV Academy.

I am hopeful that within a few short months I will have obtained the skills needed to take on more ambitious projects, be proficient at what I do, and continue to grow. Who knows I might just end up with the right skills you are looking for. Whatever it may be I would like to hear from you. Let me know how I can be of service.

When I'm not in front of a computer hacking, chances are you might spot me having a nice, long stroll with friends and/or family on the sunny beaches of Southern California.
